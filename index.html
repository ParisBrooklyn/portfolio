Jets.com

Aviation · brand transformation

Jets.com came to us mid rebrand: new look, new voice, every channel touched at once, email, social, display, content. The centerpiece was "Limitless Horizons," a cinematic hero film that repositions Jets.com as a trusted partner in life's most meaningful journeys, from the app to the aircraft, every detail rebuilt for a brand ready to lead where private aviation is headed. A team effort: I wore both hats on this one, in-house producer and account lead, booking the shoot, briefing the crew, and managing delivery specs across every format, week over week.

Great Jones Distilling

Spirits · social content, activations & partnerships

Great Jones ran alongside Bushmills and Jets.com, same seat: producing the content and holding the client relationship across a six- to seven-figure account. Studio shoots for the product range, on-location for everything else: a dedicated out-of-home campaign, a holiday pop-up bar, and a distillery partnership.

LinkNYC, out of home

We shot the Manhattan skyline from the roof of the client's building in Jersey City, then shot the bottle separately in studio, and composited the two into the final ad: "A Manhattan, actually from Manhattan." Ran across LinkNYC kiosks citywide, with copy that changed by location.

Whiskey Wonderland

A holiday pop-up bar built around the Great Jones range: full seasonal buildout, signature cocktails, a branded bar back. Content covered the space, the drinks, and the people in it.

Wölffer Estate partnership

A limited-edition Great Jones bourbon finished in Cabernet Franc casks from Wölffer Estate, the Hamptons winery. Studio product work plus on-site portraits and lifestyle at the distillery.
